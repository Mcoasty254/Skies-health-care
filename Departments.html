Our Departments

Department Name Description Details
Emergency Provides immediate care for accidents, heart attacks, strokes and other life‑threatening conditions. 24/7 emergency unit with triage, resuscitation and trauma care.
Cardiology Specializes in diseases of the heart and blood vessels. ECG, echocardiography, cardiac monitoring and heart‑failure management.
Neurology Focuses on disorders of the brain, spine and nerves. Treatment for stroke, epilepsy, headaches, movement and nerve disorders.
Pediatrics Provides complete medical care for infants, children and adolescents. Growth monitoring, vaccinations and treatment of common childhood illnesses.
Orthopedics Deals with bones, joints, muscles and sports injuries. Fracture care, joint pain treatment and post‑operative rehabilitation.
